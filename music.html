Music

I enjoy playing and listening to music a lot. I can play the guitar, piano, trombone, and the drums (not an expert, but enough to have fun lol).

Me and my friends are part of a band called Raccoon Juice. We started around 2019 with our first ever release. I've learned with them most of what I know about music. We have some great songs, so go check us out!



My favorite band at the moment is Polyphia. I've had the honor to meet them October of 2023 on their "Remember That You Will Die" tour. They are super nice people and even signed my electric guitar!
