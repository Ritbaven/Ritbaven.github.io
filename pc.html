My PC

For a long time, I have liked computers. It all started ever since a friend of mine was not playing video-games on a console, but on their desktop computer. This was confusing, so out of curiosity I wanted to give it a try in a laptop my family had. I was confused as in why this computer could not run the same games my friend was playing, if they were both the same. They weren't lol.

Ever since, I researched about gaming computers and their capabilities beyond just running video-games, which brought me to build my own PC at 19-years old (kinda late in my opinion).
